About the Lunar Research Initiative

The Lunar Research Initiative studies how quality systems, risk management, and verification methods adapt to lunar environments and highly autonomous aerospace systems.

The initiative focuses on practical tools that engineers can use now: checklists, reference architectures, and flight ready quality patterns.
